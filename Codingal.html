Monkey Habitats

Monkeys can be found in rainforests, mountains, savannas, and even cities. They adapt quickly and live in tight-knit groups called troops.

How Monkeys Communicate

Monkeys use sounds, facial expressions, and even grooming to talk to each other. Howler monkeys, for example, have super loud calls you can hear from miles away.

Banana Facts You Didn't Know

Not all monkeys love bananas! Some prefer fruits, leaves, or even insects. Capuchins have even been seen using tools to crack nuts open.
